Getting There

By Air

Fly into Jaipur International Airport (JAI), roughly a 30–45 minute drive from the venue. International guests can connect via Delhi (DEL) or Mumbai (BOM).

By Train

Jaipur Junction is well connected to Delhi and other major cities. Pre-booked taxis and ride apps are available at the station.

Good to Know

Do I need a visa for India?

Most international guests can apply for an e-Visa online. Check requirements for your nationality well in advance.

What will the weather be like?

February in Jaipur is pleasant — warm, sunny days (around 25°C) and cooler evenings. Bring a light layer for the night events.

What currency should I bring?

The Indian Rupee (INR). Cards are widely accepted in hotels; carry some cash for smaller purchases and tips.

Will there be vegetarian food?

Yes — plenty of vegetarian and other options will be available throughout.
